NLP

斋藤康毅《深度学习进阶》，笔记至LSTM。here。

[2013 - ICLR] Efficient Estimation of Word Representations in Vector Space。word2vector的开山之作，介绍CBOW以及skip-gram。

[2013 - NAACL] Linguistic Regularities in Continuous Space Word Representations。word2vector的经典问题——“queen - women + man = ？”

[2013 - NIPS] Distributed Representations of Words and Phrases and their Compositionality。上一篇论文的续作。

[2014 - EMNLP] Glove。未看。**

[2019 - NAACL] BERT: Pre-training of Deep Bidirectional Transformers for Language Understanding。BERT。

[2020 - ICLR] ALBERT: A LITE BERT FOR SELF-SUPERVISEDLEARNING OF LANGUAGE REPRESENTATIONS。一种轻量级BERT变体，ALBERT。

[2021] Roformer。旋转位置编码。未看。here。

[2021] Global Pointer。全局指针for NER。here。
